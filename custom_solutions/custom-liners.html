Custom Liners by Össur Custom Solutions

Custom silicone liners, designed by a certified prosthetist for a perfect fit.

Evolution Truly Custom Liners are designed by a certified prosthetist to fit each amputee’s unique limb shape. Truly custom throughout, from the proximal end to the distal end, both inside and out, Evolution Truly Custom Liners deliver consistent compression of the limb.

Features

  • Custom design by a certified prosthetist
  • Compatible with elevated vacuum, expulsion, and locking suspension systems
  • For patients of all lifestyles and activity levels
  • Optional wave feature for maximum comfort
  • Silky surface allows liner to slide on itself
  • Easy to don and doff
  • No coatings that can crack, peel, or wear off
  • No covers that tear or run
  • Easy to care for and keep clean

The Wave Feature

The wave feature on Evolution Truly Custom Liners reduces distal pull and bunching behind the knee for easier knee flexion and better freedom of movement.

Truly Custom

These custom liners have a custom inner diameter, outer diameter, and thickness over the entire liner, even above the knee. The benefit of being truly custom is consistent compression over the entire limb even for uniquely shaped and sensitive limbs

Advanced Medical Grade Silicone

Our medical grade silicone is naturally compatible with human tissue and resistant to fluids and bacteria, reducing the risk of infection. The liner remains fresh and odor free after each use and has lasting strength and thickness.
