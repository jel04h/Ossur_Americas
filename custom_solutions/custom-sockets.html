Why outsource socket fabrication?

Potential reasons to consider outsourcing your socket fabrication:

  • Temporarily short on techs, such as during peak busy times.
  • Make more time for complex cases by sending out easy, high volume sockets.
  • Focus more time on better documentation and patient care.
  • Offer computer-aided design services without investing in an expensive CAD system.
  • Turn fixed costs into variable costs
  • Want help with more complex, time consuming socket designs (elevated vacuum, TF, hip disartics) that you may not fabricate often.

Whatever your reasons, we’re happy to lend a hand.
