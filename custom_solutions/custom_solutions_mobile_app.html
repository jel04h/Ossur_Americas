Össur Custom Solutions iPad App

Scanning and electronic ordering for under $400!

We are proud to announce our Custom Solutions iPad App, launching April 2016, that works with a commercially available 3D sensor to scan limbs, add patient information, and send your order in minutes for ultimate convenience.

Features

  • The Össur Custom Solutions App makes ordering new sockets and custom liners a breeze.
  • Scan transtibial limbs and electronically send the information to Össur Custom Solutions in minutes. Also send measurements for AK sockets.
  • Saves hours of labor, and days of time, compared to casting and shipping casts.
  • Runs on standard iPad 2 (and later) and works with a commercially available 3D sensor.
  • Secure data transmission and storage.

iPad2 and Structure 3D sensor hardware are not included. See ordering details below.

iPad app is completely free. See below for download link to the iTunes App Store.

Get the Hardware

The Custom Solutions App runs on an iPad2 (and later). Purchase the iPad from any official local or online retailer. The commercially available 3D sensor can be purchased from Structure: https://store.structure.io/store
